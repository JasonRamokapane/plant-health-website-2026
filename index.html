🔬 Plant Health

Phytosanitary Services

We conduct rigorous plant health inspections and certifications to protect local ecosystems and meet international trade requirements.

🌾
Plant Health Inspections

Thorough on-site examinations of crops, nurseries, and consignments to detect and prevent the spread of plant diseases and pests.

📋
Phytosanitary Certificates

Official certification of plant materials for import and export compliance, meeting IPPC standards and bilateral trade requirements.

🔍
Laboratory Diagnostics

Advanced pathogen identification services using PCR, microscopy, and culture methods to accurately diagnose plant diseases.

🚢
Border Inspections

Interception and treatment of prohibited plant materials at ports of entry, safeguarding national agricultural biosecurity.

📝 Regulatory

Permits & Licensing

We guide you through the full permit lifecycle — from application to renewal — ensuring regulatory compliance at every stage.

Permit Type Description Processing Time Status
Import Permit Authorization to import plant material into the country 5–10 working days Available
Export Permit Certification for export of agricultural produce 3–7 working days Available
Agrochemical Registration Registration of pesticides and herbicides for commercial use 30–60 working days Review Required
Transit Permit Permit for transit of plant material through the country 2–5 working days Available
⚗️ Chemistry

Agrochemicals

Regulation, registration, and safe-use guidance for pesticides, herbicides, fungicides, and fertilizers to protect people, crops, and the environment.

🧪
Product Registration

End-to-end support for registering agrochemical products, including dossier preparation, safety evaluation, and regulatory submission.

⚠️
Safety Assessments

Risk evaluation of chemical inputs to ensure safe application rates, protective equipment requirements, and environmental safeguards.

📊
Residue Monitoring

Systematic testing of produce for chemical residues to ensure compliance with Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs) for local and export markets.

📚
Farmer Training

Practical workshops on responsible agrochemical handling, mixing, application, storage, and disposal for smallholder and commercial farmers.

🐛 Integrated Solutions

Pest Management

Evidence-based Integrated Pest Management (IPM) programs that minimize chemical use, protect beneficial insects, and sustain long-term crop health.

🗺️
Pest Surveillance

National and regional monitoring networks using traps, field scouting, and remote sensing to track pest populations and issue early warnings.

🦋
Biological Control

Introduction and management of natural predators, parasitoids, and biopesticides to suppress pest populations without chemical inputs.

🌿
IPM Programs

Customized integrated management plans combining cultural, mechanical, biological, and chemical strategies tailored to each farm's context.

📱
Digital Reporting

Online pest reporting portal enabling farmers and extension officers to submit sightings and receive expert guidance within 24 hours.
